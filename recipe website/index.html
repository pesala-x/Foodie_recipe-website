Kotthu Rotti

Sri Lankan Koththu Roti, also known as Kottu Roti, is a popular street food dish in Sri Lanka. It's made by chopping up roti (a type of flatbread) into small pieces and stir-frying it with a mix of vegetables, eggs, and/or meat, along with a variety of spices and sauces.
